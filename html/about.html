Our Story & Vision

Connecting landowners with buyers since 1985

From Humble Beginnings

Founded by agricultural expert Rajesh Sharma in 1985, Garuda Lands started as a small family business helping farmers find and secure productive agricultural land in Northern India.

What began as a passion project has grown into India's premier land acquisition platform. Over the decades, we've facilitated transactions for over 10,000 acres of land, helping families build legacies and investors find profitable opportunities.
